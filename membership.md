<p class="page-intro">Join LBRCC and enjoy a social road cycling club! At less than 50p a week, you get loads of benefits including our clubhouse, social activities and local discounts.</p>

We use the British Cycling online Club Management tool to manage membership. Free British Cycling membership is available for the first year, saving up to £21.00 and giving you access to all BC member benefits. If you're already a BC member there are also discounts for renewals.

<div class="callout callout-danger">
<strong>Membership is £25.00</strong> (plus £1.00 British Cycling admin fee) and renews on the same date next year.
</div>

<a href="https://www.britishcycling.org.uk/club/profile/6002/lbrcc" class="btn btn-primary" target="_blank">Join or Renew on British Cycling</a>

We always welcome new members and allow anyone to try out the club on a few rides before committing. Once you see what a great club we are, please support us by joining.

Questions? Email [lbrccmembers@gmail.com](mailto:lbrccmembers@gmail.com)

## 🎁 Membership Benefits

The following discounts are available to LBRCC members upon presentation of their membership wristband:

<div class="rules-grid" markdown="1">

<div class="rule-section" markdown="1">
### 🚲 Dorvics Cycles

[dorvicscycles.co.uk](http://www.dorvicscycles.co.uk)

Dorvics Cycles of Bridge Street, Leighton Buzzard — the oldest local bike shop in central England — is LBRCC's very own bike shop.

- Minimum 10% members discount
- Frequent club-only special offers
- Free fitting of accessories including tyres, mudguards etc.
- Free minor repairs and adjustments at the start or finish of club rides
- Loan bikes during repair
</div>

<div class="rule-section" markdown="1">

We have three bike boxes for club members to use — please book through Dorvics.
