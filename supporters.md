LBRCC continues to grow thanks to the support of our members and local businesses.

## The Black Lion

The Black Lion Public House is our "Club House". Nikki and Stev have welcomed members since they opened, providing LBRCC with facilities pre and post rides, whilst also hosting many social events including the Christmas party and Summer BBQ.

## Dorvics Cycles

<img src="/assets/images/Dorvics.jpg" alt="Dorvics Cycles" style="height: 80px; width: auto; margin-bottom: 0.5rem;">

[dorvicscycles.co.uk](http://www.dorvicscycles.co.uk)

Dorvics Cycles of Bridge Street, Leighton Buzzard — the oldest local bike shop in central England — is LBRCC's very own bike shop.

Benefits include:
- Minimum 10% members discount
- Frequent club-only special offers
- Free fitting of accessories including tyres, mudguards etc.
- Free minor repairs and adjustments at the start or finish of club rides
- Loan bikes during repair

## Kids Racing

[KidsRacing.co.uk](https://kidsracing.co.uk/) offer members a 10% discount on their range of children's cycling and triathlon clothing, along with everything else on their site.
